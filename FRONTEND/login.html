About E-CARE

E-CARE is an integrated digital healthcare platform designed to support patients with chronic illnesses such as cancer, diabetes, hypertension, and hepatitis. The system enables remote consultation, appointment scheduling, and secure medical record management, improving access and quality of care.

Our platform connects patients with verified doctors, reduces hospital waiting times, and provides a centralized solution for managing health information. E-CARE is accessible, sustainable, and complements in-person hospital services.
