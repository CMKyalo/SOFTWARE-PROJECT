Integrated Digital Care for Chronic Illness

Empowering patients and doctors with digital tools for better chronic illness management.

1. Scope of the Application / Website

The system covers online consultation, appointment scheduling, medical record management, and communication between doctors and patients with chronic diseases.
Supported illnesses: cancer, diabetes, hypertension, hepatitis.
Note: The system does not support emergency medical care or physical procedures. It complements, not replaces, in-person hospital services.

2. Reason for Choosing the Application / Website

  • Reduces long hospital waiting times via online consultation.
  • Ensures patients consult verified, qualified doctors.
  • Improves hospital management and coordination for outsourced doctors.
  • Leverages improved internet, smartphone, and power access.
  • Reduces travel and improves access for patients far from hospitals.

3. The Solution

An integrated web- and mobile-based healthcare consultation system for patients with chronic illnesses.
Features secure remote consultation, doctor verification, centralized medical records, and efficient communication.
Accessible, sustainable, and reduces unnecessary hospital visits.

4. System Requirements (Basics)

4.1 Patient Requirements
  • User registration and login
  • Profile management
  • Doctor consultation: messaging, questions, advice
  • Appointment booking
  • Medication info access
  • Health records access/uploads
  • Notifications: appointments, medication, follow-ups
  • Privacy and data protection
4.2 Doctor Requirements
  • Doctor registration and login
  • Patient management: info, medication history, multiple patients
  • Online consultation: respond, advise, update notes
  • Prescription management
4.3 System / Administrator Requirements
  • User management
  • System availability
  • Easy-to-use interface

Key Features

  • Remote consultation with verified doctors
  • Easy appointment scheduling
  • Centralized medical records management
  • Secure messaging and notifications
  • Role-based access for patients, doctors, and admins
  • Mobile and web accessibility

About E-CARE

E-CARE is designed to support patients with chronic illnesses such as cancer, diabetes, hypertension, and hepatitis. The platform enables secure, efficient, and accessible healthcare services, complementing traditional hospital care.
